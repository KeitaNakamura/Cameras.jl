Cameras.calibrate! — Method
calibrate!(camera::Camera, xᵢ => Xᵢ)

Calibrate camera from the pair of coordinates of image xᵢ and its corresponding actual coordinates Xᵢ. The elements of xᵢ should be vector of length 2 and those of Xᵢ should be vector of length 3.
