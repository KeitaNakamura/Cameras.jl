Cameras.projection_matrix — Method
projection_matrix(Xᵢ, xᵢ)

Compute the projection matrix from Xᵢ to xᵢ. The matrix $\bm{P}$ is defined in 3D as

\[\begin{Bmatrix} x \\ y \\ 1 \end{Bmatrix} = \bm{P} \begin{Bmatrix} X \\ Y \\ Z \\ 1 \end{Bmatrix}.\]

In 2D, this corresponds to homography matrix $\bm{H}$ as

\[\begin{Bmatrix} x \\ y \\ 1 \end{Bmatrix} = \bm{H} \begin{Bmatrix} X \\ Y \\ 1 \end{Bmatrix}.\]
